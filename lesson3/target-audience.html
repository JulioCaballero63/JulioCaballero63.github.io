Site Target Audience

Any person who is interested un outdoor activities. Either recreational or for work, they will be able to get the information they need to plan their day accordingly. This site will be for user of ages 8 and older. This site will be users of different back grounds and income levels.

Personas

Ray, a construction worker who does bow hunting, he does not like to get to technical about the weather but he likes to know if there are any wild fires or any other weather alerts in the area.

Mike, he is an engineer who enjoys hiking and riding his mountain bike, he will like to know how are the trail conditions, weather forecast and traffic.

Scenarios

  • Does the site show the weather in the area.
  • Does the site show wild fires in the area.
  • Does the site show traffic alerts.
  • Does the site show trail conditions.
  • Does the site show recommendations according to the user interests.
